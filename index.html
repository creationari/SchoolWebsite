St.Joseph School North Point-CAMPUS

St. Joseph's School, Darjeeling, popularly called North Point, is a private Catholic primary and secondary school for boys located in Darjeeling, West Bengal, India. Originally called St. Joseph's College, the school was founded in the year 1888 and is owned and managed by the Jesuits
